Target Audience

  The target audience of the Foothill Chamber of Commerce is made up of two major groups. The first is local business owners in the foothill area, and the other is visitors and potential residents.

Personas

Jameson

Jameson is a entrepreneur in his mid thirties trying to get his small business off the ground.

Brenda

Brenda is a mid-twenties college grad looking for a new place to live, and is interested in the local small businesses.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Jameson wants to join the Chamber of Commerce.

Scenario 2

Jameson wants to better understand the demographics of the town to better advertise his product.

Scenario 3

Brenda wants to know what kinds of businesses and organizations are in the Foothills area.

Scenario 4

Brenda wants her specific concern about possibly living in the Foothills area to be answered by the chamber.

Scenario 5

Brenda wants to know about the history of the Foothills area.
