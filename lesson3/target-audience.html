Target Audience

  The target audience of the Foothill Chamber of Commerce is made up of two major groups. The first is local business owners in the foothill area, and the other is visitors and potential residents.

Personas

Jameson

Personal

Age : 32

Gender : Male

Education : Bachelors

Professional

Jameson often worked for his father's business, doing odd chores and seeing how the business worked behind the scenes. Seeing his fathers sucess, He's decided to start his own business, although he's just a beginner. He's sought out his local Chamber of Commerce following his father's advice.

Technical

Jameson is proficeient with a computer and is well adapted to the internet. He occasionally uses his phone to browse, but prefers to wait until he's at the comfort of his home computer. He spends around an hour each day searching the internet for ways to improve his business.

Summary

Jameson is a entrepreneur in his early thirties trying to get his small business off the ground, and is looking for help and collaboration from his local Chamber of Commerce.

Brenda

Personal

Age : 26

Gender : Female

Education : Bachelors

Professional

As a student, Brenda often worked as an on-campus tutor and is interested in becoming a teacher. She's interested in vising the Foothill Chamber website to see what kind of local business are in the area, as she's considering a job in the Foothills. She heard about these kinds of website from a friend who recently moved.

Technical

Brenda has a phone and a computer, but she prefers face-to-face interactions over the internet. When browsing, she's most often on her phone, but she doesn't use technology in general very often. She tries to limit her technology use to only a few minutes at a time, and she spends about a half hour a day researching potential jobs.

Summary

Brenda is a college grad looking for a new place to live, and is interested in the local small businesses of the Foothill Area.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Jameson wants to join the Chamber of Commerce.

Scenario 2

Jameson wants to better understand the demographics of the town to better advertise his product.

Scenario 3

Brenda wants to know what kinds of businesses and organizations are in the Foothills area.

Scenario 4

Brenda wants her specific concern about possibly living in the Foothills area to be answered by the chamber.

Scenario 5

Brenda wants to know about the history of the Foothills area.
