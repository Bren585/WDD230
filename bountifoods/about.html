Our Fruit and Our People

About Our Company

Our company was founded in 1888 and is one of the oldest companies in Oceanside. We were founded on the principles of helping the fresh, healthy, and tasty fruit of our community reach the whole world. We value our customers, and espcially the cooperation and friendship of our local farmers and business owners.

About Our Produce

Our Fruit is organic and locally grown and sourced. We take pride in the quality of our product and our relationship with local farmers. For produce unable to be grown in California, we work hand in hand with local farmers in the area in which the product is grown to ensure our standards of freshness.
