Membership Levels

NP Membership

NP (Non-Profit) members have all of the perks of our Gold level members, but for no fee.

Bronze Membership

At only 20$ a month, our Bronze Members get access to our Chamber socials and our monthly newsletter.

Silver Membership

At 40$ a month, Silver Members, in addition to all the perks of Bronze Tier, are invited to participate in monthly trainings, are in the rotation for weekly spotlights on the home page.

Gold Membership

Our legendary Gold Members who pay 400$ a month are invited to participate in the all-expenses-paid bi-annual Chamber Disneyland retreat, in addition to all the perks of Bronze and Silver Membership!
